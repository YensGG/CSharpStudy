If you set your ai name to one of these,they have an added personality set ( Case sensitive ).

'Asmon', 'Zack' or 'Asmongold'

Cheats:

cheatShield         to refill shield
cheatHull           to repair hull
cheatRefuel         to refuel

cheatCargoOak    gives you 20 Oak stacks. ( Super valuable trade goods )
cheatCargo420    gives you 20 stacks of Spice Melange ( Super valuable trade goods )
